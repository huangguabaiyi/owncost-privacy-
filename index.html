中文版

概述

OwnCost（以下简称"本应用"）是一款个人资产每日成本追踪工具。我们非常重视您的隐私。

我们收集的数据

本应用不会向任何服务器发送您的个人数据。所有资产信息、消费记录均仅存储在您的设备 本地及您个人的 iCloud 账户中。

  • 本地存储：资产、消费记录、设置等数据存储在您设备的本地数据库中。
  • iCloud 同步：若您开启 iCloud 同步，数据将通过您的个人 Apple iCloud 账户在设备间同步。我们无法访问您的 iCloud 数据。
  • 汇率数据：使用多货币功能时，应用会从 frankfurter.app 获取汇率信息，该请求不包含任何个人信息。

第三方服务

  • Apple iCloud / CloudKit：用于数据同步，受 Apple 隐私政策约束。
  • Apple StoreKit：用于处理应用内购买，受 Apple 隐私政策约束。
  • Frankfurter.app：用于获取汇率数据，仅发送货币代码请求。

数据安全

您的数据完全由您掌控。您可以随时在应用设置中清除所有本地数据或 iCloud 数据。

联系我们

如有隐私相关问题，请联系：support@owncost.app

English Version

Overview

OwnCost is a personal asset daily cost tracking app. We take your privacy seriously.

Data We Collect

OwnCost does not send your personal data to any external servers. All asset information and expense records are stored only on your device and in your personal iCloud account.

  • Local Storage: Assets, expenses, and settings are stored in a local database on your device.
  • iCloud Sync: If you enable iCloud sync, data is synced through your personal Apple iCloud account. We have no access to your iCloud data.
  • Exchange Rate Data: When using multi-currency features, the app fetches exchange rates from frankfurter.app. No personal information is included in these requests.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple iCloud / CloudKit: Used for data sync, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit: Used for in-app purchases, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Frankfurter.app: Used for exchange rate data, only currency codes are sent.

Data Control

You have full control over your data. You can delete all local or iCloud data at any time from the app's Settings.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact: support@owncost.app
